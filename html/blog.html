About Me

My name is Luis Alejandro Morales, I am a Civil Engineer specialized in water resources. My experience focused on the development of hydraulics, hydrological and hydrodynamics numerical models of complex physical hydrosystems. I am particularly interested in the development of computational models to assist the design and construction of infrastructure projects in river basins. I am also interested in the assessment of climate change effects on water resources systems and in the implementation of mitigation and adaptation plans.
